# Three-party approval access guide

## Overview of third-party approval integration

Third-party approval integration, that is, Lark approval supports pushing approval tasks of external systems (**self-developed by the Company** or purchased **third-party system**) to Lark approval app. Approval tasks can reach users in real time with multiple editions (PC, mobile, Pad), and can be opened through **embedded documents of Lark approval**. Users can view and process all approvals in a unified way in Lark approval and enjoy a **one-stop** approval experience. At the same time, combined with dialog box message push, one-click forwarding chat, daily to-do reminder, efficiency kanban, and other functions, **the approval efficiency is comprehensively improved** from approval initiation, processing to statistics.

(To create processes using Lark approval, click the [Lark Approval User Manual](https://www.larksuite.com/hc/en-US/articles/360034502513))

 ## 1. Scenes

Approvals in medium and large companies are often scattered in different business systems, and approvers have to spend time switching between different systems to process approvals every day. The actions are complicated and time-consuming and approvers are prone to forget, causing consequences that business isn't processed in time and the management efficiency of the companies are jeopardized. Senior managers face more types and quantities of approval, and the problems are often more serious.

Moreover, the processing efficiency of approvers can be improved through the empowerment of daily to-do reminder, quick approval, automatic list clustering, and other approval efficiency improvement functions.

## 2. Value to integrate third-party approvals into Lark Approval Center

  1. **Integrates scattered approval documents to minimize the inconvenience of multiple entries, and creates a "one-stop" approval management center.**

        1. Integrate the documents originally scattered in **company** **OA** **, human resources, finance** and other systems to the Approval Center list, and provide unified and convenient action and query methods.
        1. The Approval Center supports embedding the approval page of the third-party system or opening it via drawers, which reduces the redirection of the approver page and brings a coherent and smooth approval experience.
        1. With simple page structure and design, the Approval Center can be used on PC, mobile, and Pad with seamless switching experience. Moreover, it can be easily promoted and started.

2. **Empowers business efficiency and contributes to a more efficient approval transfer**

    1. The approval transfers are aggregated into the approval efficiency kanban, and the improvement of management efficiency is visualized.

## 3. Best practice

ByteDance's approval was previously scattered in 23 systems, and now approvers can conduct "one-stop approval" via the "Approval Center":

-   The time to complete all nodes in a single approval is reduced by **48%** when comparing before and after integration to the Approval Center.
-   Aggregating and reminding approval to dos can, to a certain extent, avoid the situation that some documents are missing and can't be processed in time due to the scattered approval systems. The reading rate of messages within 24 hr has increased significantly by **6.62%**.
-    In the context that ByteDance has about **25,000+** approval tasks completed every day, the efficiency has improved significantly.

1.  **How can I integrate Lark Approval with a third-party system, is it required to copy the flow?**

-   It adopts a user-friendly method, which can only be accessed at the data level, and the page opened in Lark Approval is the document details of the third-party system. In this way, the cost of integration is extremely low, and the "**one-stop**" view experience is realized.

2.  **What types of approvals can be integrated into Lark Approval****?**

-   Whether a company builds its own approval system or procures third-party system, as long as the company has access to the approval task data, it can integrate with Lark Approval**. **Any company custom system can be integrated theoretically. Among third-party systems, our official Connector can integrate with **Weaver**OA and **SEEYON**OA at present, while other system such as **Landray** **, and Beisen** can be integrated through open capabilities.

3.  **What are the advantages of Lark approval solution compared with OA developed by companies themselves?**

-   In-depth integration with Lark suite: The company's own OA is separated from Lark suite, and is only an app added in Lark. Lark Approval, as an official app, can be integrated with Lark suite in a deeper extent, to achieve stronger connectivity, which means better experience and higher efficiency. Approval can be processed directly in messages, Docs, and emails, making approval closer to where the action is taking place.
-   Continuous optimization of approval efficiency and signal-to-noise ratio: Most companies take their own OA as a "one-shot deal", and rarely invest a lot of resources in continuous optimization after development, while the Lark approval team will continue to invest in improving efficiency and signal-to-noise ratio, aiming at creating "one of the most efficient approval systems".
